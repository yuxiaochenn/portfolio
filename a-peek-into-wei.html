A Peek into "Wei?"

Scroll Comic · Storytelling

A Peek into “Wei?” is a scroll comic piece in which I wrapped up the process and findings of my exploratory research into a topic of heritage language maintenance in the United States, looking into the Chinese American community. As an emerging media format on social media trending in many Asian countries, scroll comics dig into people’s everyday chores, and unfold interesting stories from smart, observable angles to resonate with the general public. Throughout the rewarding jouney of working on this, I have been challenged to tackle digital storytelling focusing on creative writing, producing seamless comic scene transitions, twigging character expressions, and typography design.

I hope this is a fun piece for you as much as for me :) Happy Scrolling!
