Background Story

As a Chinese international student living in the United States, the struggle due to the lack of accessing to a wide range of fresh vegetables for Chinese style cooking is real. Not every neighborhood has a grocery place where we can buy fresh vegetables which make of the taste of traditional Chinese dishes - the taste missed by our Chinese stomach. Under this circumstance, many of us have to stick to a relatively limited range of vegetables from the nearby grocery stores, or commute to Asian or Chinese grocery stores for a larger vegetable selection, which can be costly both money-wise and time-wise.

Initially, I was also one of them who couldn't pull myself out the struggle. One day when I was browsing Xia Chu Fang mobile app, a Chinese App that provides recipes to enrich home cooking, I was attracted by an article titled with "The Vegetables You Should Shop Next Time in North American Grocery Stores." In the article, the author introduced several vegetables by comparing them with the ones usually used in Chinese cooking. Moreover, she listed multiple recipes to demonstrate how those ones we can't purchase in the States can be nicely substitued by the more accessable and affordable vegetable options in the US local grocery stores. From then on, my selection of vegetables expanded, as well as my creased interest in lifestyle, nutrition, and clean eating.

Design Solution

By designing and creating this 2D platform game - Veggiezen, I hope to use the art of game and immersive storytelling to help my Chinese peers, who live in the States, to expand their knowledge in the vegetables in their local grocery stores, enrich their home cooking options, and also encourage them to constantly explore unknowns.

Learning Outcomes

Knowledge Level

Identify unfamiliar vegetable options that are more accessible in local grocery stores, and potential recipes to expand Chinese style home cooking.

Recognize health benefits from various vegetables.

Application Level

Apply the cooking ideas in the game to real life cooking.

The Plot

One day, you (the Player) received an SOS letter from the Veggie Planet, which informed you they need your help to rescue four of their Veggiezens kidnapped by their enemies from the Greasssy Planet. You are ready to fight with the evils and save your Veggiezen friends!
