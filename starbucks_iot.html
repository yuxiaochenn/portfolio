Unlock the Potential of Starbucks IoT Water Filtration System through Dashboard Design

During Spring 2019, I was fortunate to be selected as one of the four design students to kick off a industrial-collaboration project between Starbucks and School of Informatics and Computing at IUPUI. The four months composed a rewarding journey for me to wear multiple hats, drive product design from research to interactive proof-of-concept prototypes, and iteratively craft design decisions along with client’s feedback.
